Refunds and Cancellations

Nflm.co.in hopes that you never have to cancel or return a product. At the same time, we would be there to help you whenever you do so due to certain circumstances. As a first step we are laying out our refunds cancellation policy to take care of your hassles.

ORDER HAS NOT BEEN SHIPPED YET
Any order which has not been shipped can be cancelled partially or fully. The amount paid by you would be refunded to you and should reflect in your account in 7-10 days.

ORDER WAS DAMAGED WHEN RECEIVED
We take utmost care while packaging to ensure that the product reaches you the way you had seen it on our website. However, due to situations beyond our control, the items delivered might get damaged in transit. We would be more than happy to replace or cancel your order under such circumstance. We would request you to let us know of the damage within 48 hours of receipt of order by sending an email on customercare@ nflm.co.in. We would either send you a replacement product or provide you with a coupon of equivalent amount which you can use for the next transaction. The refund would happen only after proper check of the damaged material.

INCORRECT ITEMS RECEIVED

We are proud of the robustness of our fulfilment process and the right shipment would reach the right person at the right place. In case you receive items that are different from the ones you ordered, we will ship the right items to you right away. Please send us an email at customercare@ nflm.co.in with the order ID and complaint details. We would respond to it in 48 hours. We would try and reverse pick up the product from your location wherever possible, at other locations we will try and find a mutually agreeable solution. In case, the pick-up cannot be arranged, you can return the item through a third-party courier service and Return fees will be borne by Nflm.co.in.

Refund would be processed after the item has been shipped back or picked up from customer location.

Any other reasons for cancellation or return would be handled on a case to case basis.

Following shall not be eligible for return or replacement.

Damages due to misuse of product.
Consumable item which has been used.
Products with tampered or missing serial / item code numbers.
Any product that is returned without all original packaging and accessories, items originally included with the product(s)delivered.
Refunds are made as follows: If the payment is made using a credit/debit card (or any form of net banking) then the amount is credited back to the same account from which the payment was made or the refund is made in the form of coupon codes.
