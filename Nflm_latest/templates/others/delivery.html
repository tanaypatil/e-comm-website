Delivery

Nflm.co.in ships to ALL parts of India. (Nflm.co.in doesn't deliver product Internationally)

All orders are shipped for FREE. All prices are inclusive of Tax. There are zero hidden charges when you make a purchase on Nflm.co.in. The prices listed for all the items are final and all-inclusive. The price you see on the product page is exactly what you pay.

Nflm.co.in do not do Cash on Delivery (COD).

Nflm.co.in ships on all week days (Monday to Saturday), excluding public holidays. Nflm.co.in makes best efforts to ship each item in your order within 24hrs of the order. In the unlikely event that we are not able to ship your order completely within 3 days of the order, we shall cancel the remaining unshipped part of the order, and send you an email informing you about the same. In such cases, your payment against the unshipped part of the order shall be refunded, in the manner you have made the payment. (Subject to Refund policy). Shipments can be tracked from the My Account page. To ensure that your order reaches you in the fastest time and in good condition, Nflm.co.in only ship through reputed courier agencies. Nflm.co.in partners with premium courier service providers � Professional Courier / DTDC /Vichare /Maruti /First Flight.

Nflm.co.in is committed to the safety and security of your purchase. All packages are dispatched in NFLM branded, tamper proof bags. Nflm makes best efforts to deliver the product to you by 5-7 Working days.

Certain ODA (Outskirt Delivery Area) locations which are difficult to reach, the delivery time is more than 12 days. There are instances when the seller cannot ship to some locations. This could be a consequence of general regulations or serviceability issues.
At times Nflm.co.in prefer not to ship to certain locations. This is entirely at the Nflm's discretion. If you believe that the product is not in good condition or if the packaging is tampered with or damaged, before accepting delivery of the goods, please refuse to take delivery of the package, and contact us at customercare@nflm.co.in, mentioning your order reference number. We shall make our best efforts to ensure that a replacement delivery is made to you at the earliest. (subject to refund & cancellation policy)
